Tircle

Tircle is a game I created to further my knowledge of Unity. The aim was to create a game of tag using enemy AI to try and catch you, and then reverse states to make the enemy flee.

That's My Bird

'That's My Bird' is a puzzle platformer designed to frustrate players. The general concept of the game is the player can only have 3 abilities, abilities however include:

    • Walk Right
      Walk Left
      Jump
      Double Jump
      Shoot
